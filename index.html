App Description

VR Galleria is an innovative art gallery simulator designed for the Meta Quest headset. It allows users to immerse themselves in a virtual world where they can upload and share images of photos or artwork. Users can also host their own private worlds, creating personalized art galleries. The app leverages Open-AI technology to ensure a safe environment by filtering inappropriate and explicit content. Whether you're an artist looking to showcase your work or an art enthusiast wanting to explore new creations, VR Galleria offers a unique and engaging experience.
